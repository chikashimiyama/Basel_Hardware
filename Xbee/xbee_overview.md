### Xbee Types

#### Xbee Series 1 (XBee IEEE 802.15.4)

Appropriate for one to one or one to many communication.
Easy to setup.


##### Xbee Series ZB 

Appropriate for more complex and larger network.
It is possible to route and forward messages.
